Health and Integrity

Protecting the Rights of Mother Earth. Protecting Your Own Rights.

Words From Our Founder

The Rights of Mother Earth is an organization committed to protecting the Earth by preserving rainforests, minimizing the effects of global warming, and raising environmental awareness. We believe in an environmentally sustainable future.

All of our organization’s members follow the principle of responsibility to contribute to the health and integrity of Mother Earth. We work in synergy to ensure that all humans respect and guarantee the rights of Nature. We reject Nature’s commodification.

Our Mission

01

SAVING FORESTS & WILDLIFE

Saving rainforests and nurturing thousands of plants and animals found there, especially endangered, rare species.

02

MINIMIZING THE GLOBAL WARMING EFFECTS

We are encouraging and educating people to stop wasting and misusing the natural resources of the Earth.

03

RAISING ENVIRONMENTAL AWARENESS

Create awareness of Nature and the environment, as well as the importance of recycling and using renewable resources.

Organisation

CONSERVE ENERGY AND NATURAL RESOURCES.

Environmental issues have become so complex that many individuals don’t know how to minimize their effects on Mother Earth. We create corporate actions by educating and encouraging individuals to make small changes in their everyday lives and preserve Mother Earth.
